About:

Hello! I'm null1024 (jan Nalu). I like making music, programming, and a few other things. I've been on the internet for far too long, but I haven't had a website in years. I also slightly wish I had picked a different name back in like 2004/5/6, because there are definitely at least two other people who use the name.

Oh well. I'm not changing it now. I'm close to 20 years with it, and I'm pretty sure I was first. :P

Currently, my main project is Toki te Kone, a modification of the conlang Toki Pona that alters the grammar to be a bit less ambiguous, adjusts some word definitions, and adds a handful of new words. It is very much a work in progress, but there is some work in progress documentation available.

In addition, I've worked on a lot of music over at Battle of the Bits. Most of the earlier stuff isn't that great -- I've been at this for over 15 years, but my music from about 2017 onward is much better than from before then.

Hopefully, I'll add more to this site. I've done a lot over the years, but it's quite scattered all over the place, so I'm trying to consolidate it into one place.

I added a section for blog posts, and I hope to write fairly frequently.
