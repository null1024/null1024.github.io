My profiles:

  • TIC-80 -- games I've made for the TIC-80 fantasy computer; most notably contains eyesoul, a floaty, bouncy shoot-em-up game I wrote for a game jam.
  • Twitter -- mostly retweets, but a few old projects are shown here and occasionally I'll post something; a lot of my posts involve fighting games.

  • Sketchfab -- a handful of basic 3D models that I've made with Wings3D.
